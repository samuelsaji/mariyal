You're Still the Best Decision I've Ever Made

You know what amazes me? How you became my favorite part of every day without even trying. It's in the way I catch myself smiling at my phone when you text, or how your voice instantly makes any bad day better. I love how we can talk for hours about everything and nothing, or sit in comfortable silence just enjoying each other's presence.

What I'm trying to say is – you make my life brighter in ways I never expected. When you laugh, I want to remember the sound forever. When you're going through something hard, I wish I could take the weight from your shoulders. You've become my safe place, my favorite person, and the one I want to share both the big moments and the ordinary Tuesdays with.

I miss you between heartbeats,
In the pause of my breath when your name appears on my phone,
In the way my hands remember how yours fit just right.

I want to love you in slow motion -
To savor every ordinary miracle:
The way your eyes crinkle before you laugh,
How your voice sounds sleepy at midnight,
The courage you give me just by existing.

You're my favorite hello
And my hardest goodbye.
So take my heart -
It's been yours since the first "you too."

I don't need a special day to tell you this – just know that loving you feels like the most natural and wonderful thing in the world.

LOVE YOU SOOO MUCH <3
